| `NVS-REQ-007` | SHOULD execute GStreamer rate-change events for slow-motion playback at 0.5× and 0.75× rates across H.264, HEVC, AV1, VP9, MKV, MOV, HDR, and audio-only AAC streams at frame rates from 23 fps to 60 fps without pipeline error. |

| `NVS-REQ-009` | SHALL seek to a specified forward or backward position from a paused state across H.264, HEVC, AV1, VP9 HDR, and MOV content at frame rates from 23 fps to 60 fps and resume playback from the new position without pipeline error. |

| `NVS-REQ-012` | SHOULD sustain repeated forward and backward seek operations across H.264, HLS, DASH, and AAC streams without pipeline instability, state corruption, or playback failure. |
