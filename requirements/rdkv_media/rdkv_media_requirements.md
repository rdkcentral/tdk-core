# Media Validation Suite (MVS) — Specifications

| Req ID | Test Scope |
|:-------|:--------------------------------------|
| `MVS‑REQ‑001` | SHALL decode and render standard video and audio content — including H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, HEVC (MKV container), MKV-container content, MP4-container content, MPEG-container content, MPEG-TS-container content, H.263, VP8, VP9, AV1, EC3, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, MP3-container content, and WebM-container content — emitting a playing event within the configured playback duration without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑002` | SHALL decode and render HEVC (Main10) and H.264 (Main) profile content at the specified codec profile level, emitting a playing event without profile-level decode error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑003` | SHALL decode and render HDR video content, emitting a playing event without playback error or HDR tone-mapping failure. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑004` | SHALL decode and render 4K content including AV1, DASH, HLS, and VP9 without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑005` | SHALL establish and sustain live DASH and live HLS stream playback, emitting a playing event and maintaining continuous playback for the configured duration without buffering failure. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑006` | SHALL decode and render audio-only streams including AAC, DTS, M4A-container content, MP3-container content, OGG-container content, OPUS, and WAV PCM-container content without playback error or audio discontinuity. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑007` | SHALL parse and play MPEG-DASH streams using SegmentBase, SegmentList, SegmentTemplate, and SegmentTimeline segment addressing formats without segment retrieval error or playback failure. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑008` | SHALL decode and render DASH and HLS streams across resolution tiers including 480i, 480p, 720p, 1080i, and 1080p without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑009` | SHALL play H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, HEVC (MKV container), MKV-container content, MP4-container content, AV1, VP9, EC3, and audio-only content from start to natural end-of-stream, completing the full asset duration without playback error or premature termination. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑010` | SHALL play 4K AV1, 4K DASH, and 4K VP9 content from start to natural end-of-stream, completing the full asset duration without playback error or premature termination. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑011` | SHALL select and output Stereo, Dolby Digital, and Atmos audio sound modes for DASH content without audio output error or incorrect mode application. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑012` | SHALL pause playback and resume from the paused position for H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, HEVC (MKV container), HEVC (Main10), MKV-container content, MP4-container content, AV1, VP9, EC3, AAC, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, MP3-container content, VP8, WebM-container content, DASH, HLS, MPEG-TS-container content, OGG-container content, and HDR content, resuming from the correct position without error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑013` | SHALL pause playback and resume from the paused position for 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content, resuming from the correct position without error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑014` | SHALL sustain repeated play-pause cycles for H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, HEVC (Main10), MKV-container content, MP4-container content, AV1, VP9, EC3, AAC, HDR, MPEG-TS-container content, OPUS, and OGG-container content without playback instability or resource exhaustion. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑015` | SHALL sustain repeated play-pause cycles for 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback instability or resource exhaustion. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑016` | SHALL mute and unmute audio output during active playback of H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, HEVC (MKV container), HEVC (Main10), MKV-container content, MP4-container content, AV1, VP8, VP9, EC3, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, WebM-container content, DASH, HLS, HDR, and audio-only content without playback error or residual audio after mute. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑017` | SHALL mute and unmute audio output during active playback of 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback error or residual audio after mute. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑018` | SHALL sustain repeated mute and unmute audio operations for H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, HEVC (Main10), MKV-container content, MP4-container content, AV1, VP9, EC3, and HDR content without playback instability or resource exhaustion. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑019` | SHALL sustain repeated mute and unmute audio operations for 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback instability or resource exhaustion. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑020` | SHALL set audio volume to a specified level during active playback of H.264, HEVC, AV1, VP9, WebM-container content, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, and MP3-container content without audio artefact or playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑021` | SHALL execute fast-forward at 2× playback rate for H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), H.264 (Main) profile, HEVC (MKV container), AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, MP4-container content, EC3, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, MP3-container content, WebM-container content, DASH, HLS, and HDR content without playback error or incorrect rate application. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑022` | SHALL execute fast-forward at 2× playback rate for 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback error or incorrect rate application. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑023` | OPTIONAL — execute fast-forward at 3× playback rate for H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), H.264 (Main) profile, HEVC (MKV container), AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, MP4-container content, EC3, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, MP3-container content, WebM-container content, DASH, HLS, and HDR content without playback error or incorrect rate application. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑024` | OPTIONAL — execute fast-forward at 3× playback rate for 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback error or incorrect rate application. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑025` | OPTIONAL — execute fast-forward at 4× playback rate for H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), H.264 (Main) profile, HEVC, AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, MP4-container content, EC3, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, MP3-container content, WebM-container content, DASH, HLS, and HDR content without playback error or incorrect rate application. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑026` | OPTIONAL — execute fast-forward at 4× playback rate for 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback error or incorrect rate application. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑027` | OPTIONAL — sustain repeated fast-forward trick play operations for DASH, HLS, HEVC, AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, and EC3 content without playback instability. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑028` | OPTIONAL — sustain repeated fast-forward trick play operations for 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback instability. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑029` | SHALL seek forward during active playback of H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, AV1, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, MP3-container content, VP8, VP9, WebM-container content, DASH, and HLS content, resuming from the correct advanced position without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑030` | SHALL seek backward during active playback of H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, AV1, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, MP3-container content, VP8, VP9, WebM-container content, DASH, and HLS content, resuming from the correct earlier position without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑031` | SHALL seek to an absolute position for H.264, H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), H.264 (Main) profile, HEVC, HEVC (MKV container), AV1, VP8, VP9, MKV-container content, EC3, DASH, HLS, VORBIS, OPUS, AC3, MP3-container content, and WebM-container content, resuming from the correct position without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑032` | SHALL seek to an absolute position for 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content, resuming from the correct position without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑033` | SHALL seek to a specified position during active fast-forward playback for H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), H.264 (Main) profile, HEVC, AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, and EC3 content without playback error or incorrect position after seek. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑034` | SHALL seek to a specified position during active fast-forward playback for 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback error or incorrect position after seek. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑035` | SHALL sustain repeated forward seek operations for H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, and EC3 content without playback instability or state corruption. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑036` | SHALL sustain repeated forward seek operations for 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback instability or state corruption. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑037` | SHALL sustain repeated backward seek operations for H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, and EC3 content without playback instability or state corruption. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑038` | SHALL sustain repeated backward seek operations for 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback instability or state corruption. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑039` | OPTIONAL — execute complete trick play operations — including fast-forward, seek, and playback rate changes — for H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, HEVC (MKV container), AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, EC3, and HDR content without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑040` | OPTIONAL — execute complete trick play operations — including fast-forward, seek, and playback rate changes — for 4K AV1, 4K DASH, 4K HLS, and 4K VP9 content without playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑041` | SHALL play H.264 (DASH), H.264 (HLS), HEVC, HEVC (MKV container), AV1, VP9, MKV-container content, MP4-container content, EC3, and audio-only content in continuous loop mode without playback error or resource leak between iterations. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑042` | SHALL play 4K AV1, 4K DASH, and 4K VP9 content in continuous loop mode without playback error or resource leak between iterations. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑043` | SHALL decrypt and play Widevine AES-CTR-encrypted DASH and HLS content covering AAC, AC3, EC3, AV1, HEVC, OPUS, VP8, VP9, and H.264 codecs without decryption failure or licence acquisition error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑044` | SHALL decrypt and play Widevine AES-CTR clear-lead-encrypted DASH and HLS content covering AAC, AC3, EC3, AV1, HEVC, VP9, and H.264 codecs without decryption failure or licence acquisition error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑045` | SHALL decrypt and play Widevine AES-CTR crypt-skip-byte-block-encrypted DASH and HLS content covering AAC, AC3, EC3, AV1, HEVC, VP9, OPUS, and H.264 codecs without decryption failure or licence acquisition error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑046` | SHALL decrypt and play Widevine AES-CTR multi-key-encrypted DASH and HLS content without decryption failure or licence acquisition error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑047` | SHALL decrypt and play Widevine CBCS-encrypted DASH and HLS content covering HEVC, VP9, AV1, AC3, EC3, and H.264 codecs without decryption failure. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑048` | SHALL decrypt and play PlayReady AES-CTR-encrypted DASH and HLS content covering AAC, EC3, AV1, HEVC, and H.264 codecs without decryption failure or licence acquisition error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑049` | SHALL decrypt and play PlayReady CBCS-encrypted DASH and HLS content covering AV1, H.264, AAC, and EC3 codecs without decryption failure. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑050` | SHALL decrypt and play PlayReady multi-key-encrypted DASH and HLS content without decryption failure or licence acquisition error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑051` | OPTIONAL — execute fast-forward trick play over PlayReady AES-CTR-encrypted H.264 (DASH) and H.264 (HLS) content without decryption failure or playback error. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑052` | SHALL render simple, complex, and combined animation scenes — including rectangles, texts, images, and multiple animation objects at varying object counts — with average FPS meeting expected thresholds. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑053` | SHALL render animation scenes with average device CPU load and graphics workload not exceeding expected thresholds. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑054` | SHALL execute animation state operations meeting expected throughput thresholds. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑055` | SHALL sustain repeated animation play-pause cycles without playback instability or resource exhaustion. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑056` | SHALL sustain repeated animation start/stop cycles without playback instability or resource exhaustion. |
| `MVS‑REQ‑057` | SHALL confirm animation rendering accuracy for rectangle and text object animations via screenshot comparison. |
